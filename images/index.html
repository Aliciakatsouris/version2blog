Hey! My name is Alicia, and I am a 19-year-old journalist and musician now based in New York City. Currently, I am a sophomore at The New School University majoring in Journalism + Design. I had my first few big interviews (sought out on my own) last year related to Rock music, which are featured on my Writing and Reporting page. My favorite interview is with Charles Peterson, Kurt Cobain's photographer and one of the greatest underground photographers of the 90s and Grunge era. My style is authentic, bold, and fun. I am hoping to intern soon to experience more insight and continue to be attentive in my work of musicians and subjects not only in the mainstream! I know that I would fit right in at Spin.
